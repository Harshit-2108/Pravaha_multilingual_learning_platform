About Pravaha

Pravaha is India’s first AI-powered multilingual localization engine designed to make education inclusive and accessible. By bridging the language divide, we help millions of learners access skill-based content in their own mother tongue.

Our Mission

To democratize access to education by providing high-quality localized content in all major Indian languages. We aim to empower rural learners and bridge the gap between technology and inclusivity.

Our Vision

A future where language is no longer a barrier to learning. Pravaha envisions an India where every learner can grow, skill up, and thrive in their own language, making education truly accessible to all.
